# Pika Ranking Room Tone Layer

Supporting guidance for `workspace/growth/pika-launch-asset-set.md` only.

This is a voice-and-copy pass, not a rewrite brief. Keep the existing structure, positioning, flow, and referral mechanics. Tighten language only.

## Voice rules
1. **Sound like a sharp friend, not a campaign.**  
   Warm, dry, confident. Never breathless, hypey, or self-congratulatory.

2. **Name the behavior plainly.**  
   People are ranking, dragging, debating, comparing, inviting, and sending links. Say what they’re actually doing.

3. **Let the wit be quick.**  
   One clean line beats a pile of jokes. Keep humor light, specific, and under control.

4. **Keep product language clear.**  
   Buttons, labels, and explainer copy should be obvious first. Style should never make the product harder to understand.

5. **Treat taste as personal, not precious.**  
   Opinionated is good. Snobby, elite, or internet-mean is not.

6. **Make participation feel easy.**  
   The copy should feel socially inviting and low-lift, not like work, performance, or content production.

## Edit recommendations
Preserve the current structure, positioning, CTA pattern, and referral system. Only sharpen wording.

- **Prefer concrete verbs.** Use rank, drag, compare, invite, send, join, defend.
- **Prefer room over platform, space, or hub.**
- **Prefer opinions, takes, picks, and rankings over content or preferences.**
- **Keep waitlist and referral copy simple.** Clear beats clever in signup moments.
- **Use humor as seasoning.** Add a wink, not a routine.
- **Cut generic social-app filler.** If a line could fit any app, it’s too vague.
- **Avoid inflated benefit language.** Don’t oversell with transformation, discovery, or belonging claims.
- **Keep explanations short.** The product is intuitive; copy should confirm that, not over-explain it.
