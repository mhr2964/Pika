# Progress Update on the Implementation of the Revised Action Plan for Pika Platform Project

## Update on Action Steps

### 1. Define Roles and Responsibilities
- **Status**: Completed
- **Details**: The Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RACI) document has been finalized and distributed to all project teams. Each team lead has confirmed understanding and acceptance of their roles.

### 2. Establish Regular Cross-Departmental Meetings
- **Status**: Ongoing
- **Details**: Bi-weekly cross-department check-ins have commenced. The first meeting went well, with key action items identified and discussions around initial challenges. The next meeting will follow up on these action items.

### 3. Conduct a Workstream Audit
- **Status**: In Progress
- **Details**: The audit is under way, with team members collecting data on their current projects. We anticipate having a consolidated report by the end of next week that will outline overlaps and possible consolidations.

### 4. Improve Documentation Practices
- **Status**: Initiated
- **Details**: A centralized documentation repository has been set up in Notion. Teams have been informed and training sessions are being organized to ensure compliance with the new standards by the end of the month.

### 5. Create a Technical Debt Taskforce
- **Status**: Formed
- **Details**: The Technical Debt Taskforce is now active, with representatives from the backend, frontend, and devops departments. The first meeting is scheduled for this week to identify specific areas of technical debt to address.

### 6. Set Measurable Objectives and KPIs
- **Status**: Drafting Phase
- **Details**: Draft KPIs have been shared for feedback among project teams. Final amendments will be made after gathering input, with the aim to finalize them by the end of the week.

### 7. Review and Iterate Action Plan
- **Status**: Pending
- **Details**: We are preparing for the first review of the implementation plan in six weeks. Teams are reminded to keep documentation of their progress for this review.

## Next Steps
- Continue regular bi-weekly check-ins and ensure follow-ups on action items.
- Finalize the workstream audit next week and share findings with stakeholders.
- Gather feedback and finalize the KPIs.
