# Pika Post-Signup Referral Screen

## Recommended Primary Copy

**Headline:** You’re on the waitlist. Now bring your people.  

**Body:** Your spot is saved. Want to move up? Share your personal invite link with friends who always have a ranking, a favorite, or a strong disagreement. When they join through your link, you climb the list.  

**Primary CTA:** Copy my invite link  

**Secondary CTA:** Share with friends

## Approved Alternate Support Line

**Support line:** Don’t just wait for access. Move up by bringing in the people whose opinions you actually want in the room.

## Usage Notes

- Keep the framing: **build your room, don’t collect referrals.**
- Use this on waitlist confirmation pages, post-signup modals, or success states.
- Do not add rewards, points, giveaway language, or generic referral bait.
