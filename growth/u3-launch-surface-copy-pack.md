# U3 Launch Surface Copy Pack

## Canonical Rule
Use this exact top-line hook on primary surfaces:

**Build your room. Rank with friends.**

Supporting framing may only use:
- Build your room
- Rank with friends
- Compare picks
- See where everyone lands
- Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room

---

## 1) Landing

### Headline
**Build your room. Rank with friends.**

### Subhead
Build your room, rank with friends, compare picks, and see where everyone lands.

### Body
Create your room with the people you actually want in it, then rank with friends and compare picks together.

### CTA
Join the waitlist

### Optional reinforcement line
Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room.

---

## 2) Waitlist / Referral

### Headline
**Build your room. Rank with friends.**

### Subhead
Build your room, rank with friends, and compare picks.

### Body
Join the waitlist to build your room, compare picks, and see where everyone lands.

### CTA
Join the waitlist

### Referral explainer
Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room.

### Referral follow-up line
Your place can move up when the people you invite join the waitlist.

### Referral CTA
Invite your room
