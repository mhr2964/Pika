# U3 Message Lock Application Map

## Canonical Rule
The required top-line hook across active U3 acquisition and referral surfaces is:

**Build your room. Rank with friends.**

Approved supporting framing may only use:
- Build your room
- Rank with friends
- Compare picks
- See where everyone lands
- Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room

## Surface Rules

### Clean
Use `clean` when the surface can support:
1. The exact top-line hook first
2. One approved supporting line beneath it
3. A clear CTA or referral action beneath the support line

### Constraint
Use `constraint` when the surface has format or character limits that may prevent full hierarchy from appearing at once. On constrained surfaces:
- Keep the exact hook first whenever possible
- Reduce supporting copy before changing the hook
- Do not introduce alternative positioning to compensate for lack of space
- Keep referral language clear if waitlist movement is mentioned

## Approved Slot Examples

### Supporting Copy Slot Examples
- Build your room, rank with friends, and compare picks.
- Compare picks and see where everyone lands.
- Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room.

### CTA / Referral Language Slot Examples
- Join the waitlist
- Invite your room
- Join my room
- Join the room
- Your place can move up when the people you invite join the waitlist.

## Hierarchy Enforcement Notes
- The hook must remain unchanged: **Build your room. Rank with friends.**
- Supporting alternatives may only appear below the hook.
- No surface may introduce a competing frame above the hook.
- If a constrained surface cannot carry the full hierarchy, shorten the supporting copy or CTA line first.
- Waitlist movement language should appear only where invite mechanics or referral explanation are present.
