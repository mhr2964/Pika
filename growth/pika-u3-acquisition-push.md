- **Primary share CTA:** Share with friends
- **Secondary share CTA:** Move up by inviting friends

- **Referral card line:** Bring in friends with taste, takes, and zero hesitation.

Use only this framing: **build your room, don’t collect referrals.**

Avoid generic incentive language like rewards, bonuses, points, exclusives, or giveaways.

## 4) Locked Usage Notes
