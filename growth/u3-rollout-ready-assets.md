## Compliance Notes

- **Primary Hook Rule:** "Build your room. Rank with friends." appears first and most prominent on all acquisition, waitlist, referral, and share-loop surfaces.
- **Supporting Copy:** All approved supporting lines sourced from acquisition pack sections 1–3 and appear subordinate only.
- **Waitlist-Movement Language:** "Move up the waitlist by inviting people you actually want in the room" and "Your place can move up when the people you invite join the waitlist" appear only on referral invite screens, confirmation states, and referral explainers where invite mechanics are present.
- **Compressed Surfaces:** Canonical hook + one approved supporting line; hook remains first on all placements.
- **No Testing Against Primary Hook:** The exact hook is locked and may not be rotated or tested on primary rollout surfaces.
- **Surface Hierarchy:** All surfaces conform to brand hierarchy compliance addendum section 5 surface allowance table.
