# Pika Top-Channel Outreach Set

## Recommended Primary Copy by Surface

### 1. Group Chat / Text
**Primary:** I joined the Pika waitlist — it’s built for ranking with friends and comparing picks. You were one of the first people I thought of. Join with my link?

### 2. X / Twitter
**Primary:** Your group chat has opinions. Pika gives them a ranking room. I’m on the waitlist — if you rank everything and defend your picks, get on here with me.

### 3. Instagram Story
**Primary frame copy:** I joined Pika. It’s a ranking room for people who actually have opinions.  
**Primary sticker line:** Join me on the waitlist.

### 4. Discord / Community Channel
**Primary:** Found a waitlist that actually fits this server: Pika is a ranking room for comparing picks, arguing order, and seeing where everyone lands. I joined — if this sounds like your thing, use my link.

### 5. 1:1 DM Outreach
**Primary:** Pika made me think of you immediately. It’s basically a ranking room for people who always have a top 5 ready. I joined the waitlist — want my link?

## Usage Notes

- Prioritize direct channels first: group chat, text, and 1:1 DM.
- Use public channels to reinforce identity: ranking, opinions, taste, disagreement.
- Keep all outreach aligned to: **build your room, don’t collect referrals.**
