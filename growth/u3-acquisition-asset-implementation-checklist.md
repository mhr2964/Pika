## Constraint Handling Rules
- If hierarchy cannot be maintained cleanly, shorten supporting copy before changing the hook.
- Do not test or introduce alternative positioning above the hook.
- Keep waitlist movement language only on assets that include referral or invite mechanics.
- On constrained channels, preserve the exact hook first whenever possible, then retain the clearest invite or waitlist explanation line.

## Final QA Before Launch
- Primary hook is exact: **Build your room. Rank with friends.**
- Supporting copy stays within the approved U3 frame.
- CTA and referral variants come only from the cleared acquisition pack.
- No asset introduces a competing headline, promise, or positioning frame.
- Waitlist movement is explained clearly anywhere referral language appears.
